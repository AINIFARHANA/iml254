Higher Education!

Following my graduation from high school, I enrolled in a diploma program in library informatics. I began my university education at Univeristi Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Kedah in 2022. It has been an enriching journey filled with new knowledge, challenges, and growth. I chose a diploma in informatic library because it combines my passion for technology with my interest in organizing and managing information efficiently. From exploring diverse subjects to engaging in extracurricular activities, my time here has truly shaped my academic and personal development. I'm in my fourth semester now, and I can keep my pointer sharp. I'm attempting to learn things here that I haven't tried before, for instance, using Adobe, cataloging books, and coding. Focused learning has been the cornerstone of my university education, as I devote my attention to every lecture, seminar, and workshop to acquire a comprehensive understanding of my chosen field. I am excited to take the next step in my academic journey and pursue a degree in informatic library science, expanding my knowledge and expertise to new heights.
