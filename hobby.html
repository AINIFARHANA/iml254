INGREDIENTSCHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

NO. INGREDIENTS INSTRUCTION
Quantity Description
1 Granulated Sugar 1 cup (200g) Granulated sugar to provide sweetness.
2 Unsalted butter, softened 1 cup (226g) Adds richness and helps create a tender, moist texture.
3 Brown sugar 1 cup (220g) Contributes to their chewiness and imparts a subtle caramel flavor.
4 Eggs 2 eggs Act as a binding agent and help provide structure and moisture to the dough, resulting in a soft and chewy texture.
5 Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon Adds flavor and aroma.
6 Flour 3 cups (360g) Helps absorb moisture, giving the cookies their desired texture and consistency.
7 Baking Soda 1 teaspoon Helping the cookies rise and giving them a soft and fluffy texture.
8 Salt 1 teaspoon Balancing sweetness and intensifying the richness of the chocolate.
9 Chocolate chips 2 cups (340g) Creamy chocolate flavor and delightful texture, making each bite a delicious treat.
